About Us

LexStart is a platform that provides legal and compliance services exclusively to the start-up ecosystem. LexStart is the brainchild of a VC and a seasoned lawyer, who amongst them have advised several start-ups and worked on quite a few investment transactions to know where exactly the pain points and pitfalls are. It is this experience that the team LexStart brings to the table to ensure that a startup is investment ready at all times!

We have been there done that! We believe that every start up is unique and hence needs customized solutions. LexStart representatives are here to bounce of ideas and concerns and be founder's sounding board.
