Did you know that there is a mandatory 1 year cliff on ESOPs?

A cliff is the time period between grant of ESOPs to an employee and vesting of the ESOPs. Under the Indian Companies Act, 2013, there has to be a mandatory one year gap between granting and vesting of ESOPs. Why does it matter?

It matters because in most start ups, the promoters tend to offer ESOPs to employee as part of the compensation in the offer letter. A promise to give
ESOPs in an offer letter is not “granting.”
